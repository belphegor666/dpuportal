NHSS TeleHealth

The challenge

How can NHS Scotland make use of mobile technology to increase the efficiency of their care provision and improve patient care?

The answer

A demonstration application that would improve communication with patients, increase efficiency of the communication process and reduce the number of missed appointments.

The solution was an iOS Mobile app which addressed the challenges of a simple business use case and provided a platform which could be built upon to further improve patient care and provide more efficiency savings.

The WOW features


Integration with patient's own calendar reduces the chances the appointment is missed.


Instant notification of new appointments direct to patient's phone - no need to send out a letter.


Patient can immediately confirm or re-arrange an appointment, sending acknowledgement to the care provider.


Strong branding and user experience demonstrates quality and develops trust from the user.


Follows the paradigms of the platform so it's intuitive to use.

DPU Process

  • End to end demonstration app delivered in 3x 1 week sprints, including a discovery phase.
  • Rapid prototyping was used as a quick start for development, allowing functionality to be demonstrated to the Product Owner quickly.
  • Multiple feedback loops per sprint with the Product Owner to 'course correct'.
  • Delivered early to support a sales conference and distributed via an internal App Store.
