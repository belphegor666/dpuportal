MoD Visitor Booking System

The challenge

In two weeks, to create a working protoype that articulated a better future and the art of the possible.

The answer

Working with a team from across MoD, we created a live service that showed a better way was possible. The output is supporting MoD in defining their requirements for a new system which will be procured in 2016.

Convenience, efficiency and
better data

A new workflow was tested - visitors could initate a vist by entering their details and submitting the request. MoD staff can accept or decline - no need to enter visitor details.

The WOW features

As well as the usual visitor booking features, the protoype implemented capabilities to improve control and operational efficiency.

Visitors numbers can be easily seen for each day to allow establishments to resource security and reception appropriately.

Integrated with Calendars Accepted meeting requests are synchronised to the visitor and MoD staff's calendars.

VIP Visitors highlighted for reception and Head of Establishment.

Text Message on Visitor Arrival with variants for needs escorting and security cleared.

Establishment Security can see if banned persons are trying to access a site.

Security Clearance details are stored and can be verified by reception and security staff.

The Digital Prototyping Unit process

The process started with discovery workshops to understand and document user needs.

Capturing requirements a half day workshop was run to capture high level requirements.

Trello was to turn requirements into user stories. Trello was also used for detailed sprint planning and progress reporting.






Weekly sprints deliver working software quickly. Our aim is a production ready update at least once a week. Project sponsors can attend any of the team and project meetings.

Virtual teams & distributed Agile allow us to leverage the skills of Atos UK and make progress most quickly.
